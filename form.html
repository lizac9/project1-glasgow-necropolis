Book a Tour

Any tour of the Necropolis, Glasgow's garden cemetery based on Mount Louis, Pere Lachaise in Paris, should advisedly start and finish at the St Mungo's Museum of Religious Life and Art in the Cathedral precincts - which is the UK's only museum celebrating the world's many religions. St Mungo's museum features a priceless collection of art and religious artefacts and contains an enchanting Zen garden. Daily guided tours of the Necropolis leave from this point, refreshments and toilets are available.
